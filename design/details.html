Tour Detail

  • 1
    HO CHI MINH ARRIVAL (-/-/D)

    Your guide will meet you at the airport and escort you to the hotel. Full day of the city will be provided in the afternoon: the Reunification Palace, one of the most important buildings of the city, the former residence of the President of South Vietnam until April 30, 1975, the date of the end of war against the United States. 


    The War Museum, formerly known as the Museum of American war crimes, contains a gallery called 'Requiem' and is dedicated to Vietnamese photographed by foreign journalists during the conflict. The exhibition also features former bombing tanks and rusty cannon U.S. Army. 

    The Notre Dame Cathedral and Old Post Office (built between 1877 and 1883, it is one of the finest examples of French colonial architecture). Each stone used in its construction was imported from France.

    Giac Lam Pagoda: the oldest pagoda in Ho Chi Minh, built in 1744. It has 98 pillars, 113 statues and a myriad of mini Buddhas. Don't miss the tree of wandering souls, where people pray for their loved ones by writing their names on papers they attach to the tree.

    Ho Chi Minh City is currently the most modern city in Vietnam and has all the amenities of a big city with shopping malls, cinemas, restaurants and nightclubs. Enjoy a walk along the rue Catinat Ex- for pictures in front of the Opera House and then the Town Hall, built by the French.

    Evening and welcome dinner at local restaurant. Overnight in Ho Chi Minh.

  • 2
    HO CHI MINH - NHA TRANG (B/L/D)

    After breakfast, our driver will transfer to the airport for the flight to Nha Trang.

    Cruise in Nha Trang Bay. You will visit Tri Nguyen Aquarium on Mieu Island, where you will see hundreds of sea creatures as beautiful as precious. Continue your discovery on Mun Island, best of Vietnam for diving. Let yourself be enchanted by the charm of underwater life with its various colorful fish and corals.

    Lunch on Con Se Tre Island before returning to the hotel in Nha Trang.

    Overnight at hotel in Nha Trang. 

  • 3
    NHA TRANG - BEACH EXTENSION (B/L/D)

    Have breakfast in your hotel. 

    Dive into the coral reef in the turquoise water, walk through the nature trails, cool off in your private pool, take care or relax on one of the most beautiful beaches in Viet Nam white sand. 

  • 4
    NHA TRANG - BEACH EXTENSION (B/L/D)

    Breakfast at hotel, free day to enjoy your trip private wedding.

  • 5
    NHA TRANG – HA NOI (B/L/D)

    After breakfast at the hotel, our driver transfert you to the airport for flight to Hanoi, artistic and intellectual capital, with its tree-lined boulevards, colonial architecture, peaceful lakes, pagodas and its oriental temples.

    Explore the fascinating Old Quarter and its hundreds of specialty shops selling silk clothing, lacquer ware, embroidered linens, furniture, bamboo, ceremonial objects and more ... Also visit the shops of modern art, without forgetting the main cultural attractions : including the Fine Arts Museum and the Temple of Literature, Tran Quoc Pagoda and Quan Thanh Temple.

    Evening puppet show on the water, unique in the world before dinner. A peaceful night in your hotel in Hanoi.
